Fortify Your Use of Generative AI with SkyDeck.ai

Introduction

In the cloud era, security is as vital to businesses as profitability. The increasing interconnectivity and digitalization of operations mean that more and more sensitive data are stored and transferred in the cloud. To protect this valuable information, businesses require robust, secure business tools. You also need to fortify your use of Generative AI. This is where SkyDeck.ai, comes in, providing an enterprise-ready AI integration that prioritizes the security of your data and operations.

Fortify Your Use of Generative AI with Enterprise-Ready Security

SkyDeck.ai is designed to meet the stringent security demands of modern businesses. As an enterprise-ready platform, it has been built with a robust infrastructure that can handle large volumes of data & users while ensuring optimal performance and AI security. SkyDeck is also committed to gaining certifications SOC2, ISO27001, CCPA, and GDPR, starting with SOC 2. This makes it a reliable choice for businesses of all sizes, from startups to large corporations, seeking to adopt an AI strategy.

Fortify Your Use of Generative AI with Data Encryption at Rest and in Motion

To safeguard your sensitive AI database, SkyDeck.ai utilizes advanced encryption methods. This includes AES 256-bit encryption for data at rest, one of the most secure encryption methods available today. It uses a 256-bit key to encrypt and decrypt data, providing a high level of security that is currently unbreakable by brute-force attacks.

For data in motion, SkyDeck.ai employs Transport Layer Security (TLS) 1.2 and above. This protocol ensures that data moving between your systems and SkyDeck.ai is secure and cannot be intercepted or altered.

Fortify Your Use of Generative AI with Data Loss Prevention (DLP)

Data loss can be detrimental to any business. SkyDeck.ai‘s DLP feature helps prevent this by monitoring and controlling sensitive data. It identifies potential breaches and prevents them by enforcing pre-set policies, ensuring that your valuable data remains within the safe confines of your AI enterprise. Sensitive Data is identified and redacted before sending to your LLM, or can be blocked.

Fortify Your Use of Generative AI with Single Sign-On (SSO)

SkyDeck.ai‘s Single Sign-On feature provides a balance between ease-of-access and IT security. SSO allows users to log in once and gain access to multiple applications, eliminating the need for multiple passwords and reducing the risk of password-related security breaches. Email, Asure AD and Google are supported now, with many more coming soon.

Fortify Your Use of Generative AI with Logging and Analytics

Visibility is key in AI security. SkyDeck.ai provides comprehensive logging and analytics features, allowing you to monitor activity on the platform. This helps in identifying any unusual or suspicious patterns, enabling quick action to mitigate potential threats.

Fortify Your Use of Generative AI with Private Models

SkyDeck.ai recognizes that each business has unique security requirements. Hence, it offers the option to use private models. This feature allows you to customize the AI tools and models you use, ensuring they align with your business’s specific IT security needs and standards. SkyDeck.ai also integrates with Hugging Face for secure access to private models.

Curation: Manage Access Effectively with SkyDeck.ai

SkyDeck.ai provides a practical feature for owners and admins: the ability to curate access to different resources. This means they can specify which members or users can access various AI models, smart prompts, smart tools, and smart agents.

This curation ability is a useful aspect of AI business tools. It facilitates the alignment of resources to specific team tasks, ensuring optimal usage and reducing the potential for misuse. With this feature, businesses can allocate the appropriate resources to the right individuals or teams, thereby enhancing overall productivity.

Curation: Examples

For example, an admin can grant a data analysis team access to specific AI models and smart tools relevant to their work. In contrast, a different access level could be set for a team working on strategy, providing them with a different set of AI models and smart prompts tailored to their needs.

This customized approach not only ensures effective resource utilization but also contributes to the security of your AI infrastructure. By managing access to various AI models and tools, admins can prevent unauthorized usage, adding an extra layer of security.

In summary, SkyDeck.ai‘s curation feature provides businesses with the ability to fine-tune their AI experience, ensuring resources are used effectively, security is maintained, and every team has the tools they need to perform their tasks.

Conclusion

In today’s digital landscape, securing your business applications is crucial. SkyDeck.ai is committed to providing an AI platform that not only enhances productivity but also prioritizes security. With features like robust data encryption, DLP, SSO, comprehensive logging and analytics, and the option for private models, SkyDeck.ai ensures your business is equipped with a secure, efficient, and adaptable AI platform. Take control of your data security with SkyDeck.ai and fortify your use of generative AI.
